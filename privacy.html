Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nate Templeton ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the Website Accountability Challenge (WAC 2026) program and website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Sign up for the free trial or paid round via our forms or Stripe
  • Submit weekly proof of website edits
  • Contact us directly via email or our contact form

This may include your name, email address, website URL, and payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we do not store your card details).

How We Use Your Information

  • To enroll and manage your participation in WAC 2026
  • To track and verify your weekly edit submissions
  • To communicate program updates, reminders, and results
  • To determine prize eligibility
  • To improve the program in future rounds

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Google — if you submit via Google Forms. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Service providers who assist in operating the program, under confidentiality obligations.

Data Retention

We retain your information for the duration of your participation and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping and prize administration. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To do so, email us at the address on our contact page.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics at this time.

Children's Privacy

WAC 2026 is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the program evolves. Material changes will be communicated to active members by email.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Visit our contact page.
