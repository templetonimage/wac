FAQ

Common Questions

What platform does my website need to be on?
Any platform counts — Squarespace, Showit, WordPress, Webflow, Wix, custom-built. If you can edit it and screenshot it, it qualifies.
How do I submit proof of my edits?
We're building a custom WAC Platform where you'll be able to upload proof, track your submissions, view your streak, and access strategy and education resources — all in one place. Early access details will be shared with trial and Round 1 members in the coming weeks. Stay tuned.
What happens if I miss a week?
You stay in the challenge and can keep posting, but you lose WAC All-Star status and the All-Star cash prize for that round. You're still eligible for the random finisher drawing as long as you complete the round. Every week counts.
How does the cash giveaway work?
There are two prizes per round. The All-Star prize goes to members who hit every weekly minimum — zero misses. The finisher prize is a random drawing open to anyone who completes the round, regardless of whether they hit all requirements. Both prizes grow as membership grows. Full details will be shared at the start of each paid round.
Is WAC related to SMAC?
Yes — WAC is the sister challenge to SMAC (Social Media Accountability Challenge), created by the same person with the same structure. SMAC focuses on social media consistency; WAC focuses on website updates. You can join one or both.
When does Round 1 start and end?
The 30-day free trial runs throughout April 2026. Round 1 (the first paid round) runs May through July 2026. Enrollment details for each will be announced closer to the start date.
